Tornadoes

Tornadoes are violently rotating columns of air that extend from a thunderstorm to the ground.

Alternate names: Twisters, Whirlwind, Cyclone

HOW TO PREPARE FOR IT:

  1. Immediately go to a safe location that you identified.
  2. Take additional cover by shielding your head and neck with your arms and putting materials such as furniture and blankets around you.
  3. Listen to EAS, NOAA Weather Radio, or local alerting systems for current emergency information and instructions.
  4. Do not try to outrun a tornado in a vehicle.
  5. If you are in a car or outdoors and cannot get to a building, cover your head and neck with your arms and cover your body with a coat or blanket, if possible.
  6. Store important documents, just in case.
  7. Set up means of communication.
  8. Know the closest evacuation center.
  9. Create an emergency kit.
  10. Build or designate a safe room.
  11. Arrange and secure household items.

WHY IS IT A THREAT?
